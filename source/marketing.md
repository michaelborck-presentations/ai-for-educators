## About the Speaker: Dr. Michael Borck

Michael Borck is an experienced professional in information systems, with expertise in machine learning, software engineering, and computer vision. His journey began at the Queensland University of Technology (Mathematics), continued at the Australian National University (Computer Science), and Curtin University (Graduate Diploma in Business). In September 2017, Dr. Borck completed his PhD focused on feature extraction from multi-modal mobile mapping data.

Over his career, he has served as Software Developer, Consultant, Associate Lecturer, IT Manager, Analyst/Programmer, and System Administrator. Dr. Borck is a Certified Professional of the Australian Computer Society and an active member of the IEEE Computer Society. He has co-founded the Business AI Research Group, aiming to leverage business innovation through advanced AI research.

## Session Overview

This 60-minute session will help you:

* Understand AI as a tool for your teaching, not a threat to it
* Learn practical prompting techniques you can use immediately
* Explore assessment design strategies for the AI era
* Develop confidence in using AI thoughtfully and transparently

## Topics Covered

* **The Real Barrier:** Addressing AI shame and the fear of irrelevance
* **The Core Concept:** At the heart of ALL AI are instructions
* **Three Techniques:** Board of Directors, Devil's Advocate, and Reverse Prompting
* **Assessment Reality:** If AI can do your assignment, what does that mean?
* **Ethics & Next Steps:** The Billboard Rule and your one commitment

* A personalised learning path based on your experience level
* One concrete experiment to try this week
